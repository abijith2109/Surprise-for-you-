My Love

From the day you walked into my life, everything started to feel warmer, brighter, and more meaningful. You are not just someone I love—you are the peace in my chaos, the smile in my hardest days, and the reason my heart feels full.

When I think about my future, I don’t see places or things. I see you— walking beside me, laughing with me, growing old with me. I want to be there for every small moment and every big dream, to hold your hand through happiness and through storms.

If life is a long journey, I don’t want to walk even a single step without you. I want my story to begin with you, continue with you, and gently reach its final chapter with you still beside me.

You are my today, my tomorrow, and the love I want until the very end of my life.
